Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  A2e Technologies, San Diego, CA

✅ Contracts Manager collects no personal information. All data you enter stays on your Mac. Nothing is transmitted to us or any third party.

What Information We Collect

We collect no personally identifiable information (PII) of any kind. Contracts Manager does not ask for, store, or transmit your name, email address, phone number, payment information, or any other personal data.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Contracts Manager — including recipient names, contract details, FAR/DFAR requirements, and notes — is stored locally on your Mac in:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/ContractsManager/

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a backup file using the Save Database feature, which saves a JSON file to a location you choose.

Analytics & Tracking

Contracts Manager does not include any analytics SDK, crash reporter, or telemetry of any kind. We do not track how you use the app.

Third-Party Services

Contracts Manager does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or cloud platforms. No data is shared with any external party.

macOS Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the app schedules local macOS notifications (e.g., 45 days before contract expiration). These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device by macOS — no data is sent to our servers or anyone else.

Children's Privacy

Contracts Manager is a professional business tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date above. Since we collect no data, changes are expected to be infrequent.
